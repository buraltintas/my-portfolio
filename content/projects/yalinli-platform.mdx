## Highlight

## Overview

Yalınlı Platform is a neighborhood-focused civic product designed to collect local problems, suggestions, events, and petitions in a structured way. It supports public submissions, anonymous reporting, multilingual content, petition signatures, exportable reports, and a BFF-secured backend architecture.
